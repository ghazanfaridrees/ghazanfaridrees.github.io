About

I didn't start with a thesis about Muslim mental health. I started with watching people I grew up with avoid therapy — not because they didn't need it, but because nothing felt safe, culturally understood, or affordable.

That observation became a question. The question became research. And the research showed a market: 1.8 billion Muslims globally, chronically underserved, with a trust gap no generic platform has closed.

So I stopped being an employee. I co-founded Shifa Therapy and CounselClinic — two online therapy platforms built from zero in 18 months. 165+ verified therapists. Clients across 20+ countries. Organic traffic up 1,213% without a paid acquisition budget.

I run everything — product, technology, sales, therapist operations, marketing, pricing, fundraising. Not because I have to. Because I believe the operator who understands every layer of the machine builds the most durable company.
